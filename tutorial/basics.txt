Scheme Basics
=============

pyscheme is a small R7RS-style Scheme interpreter.  Start with
self-evaluating values:

  42            -> 42
  "hello"       -> "hello"
  #t            -> #t
  '(1 2 3)      -> (1 2 3)

Bind a name:

  (define x 5)

Call a primitive:

  (+ 1 2 3)      -> 6
  (cons 1 '())   -> (1)

Define a procedure:

  (define (square n) (* n n))
  (square 7)     -> 49

Procedures can take a docstring - the first body expression, provided
there is at least one more form after it, is treated as documentation:

  (define (double n)
    "Return twice its argument."
    (* n 2))

  (help double)

Try (help) for the full list of forms and primitives.
